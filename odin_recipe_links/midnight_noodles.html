Midnight Noodles

When late night cravings hit, midnight noodes are my fast 5 minute go to. These bad bois are a creamy luxurious take on a cheap ramen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of your go-to cheap ramen
  • kewpie mayo: 1 TBSP
  • Egg yolk: 1
  • protien of choice
  • spring onion
  • crispy onions
  • favourite oil or chili crisp
  • any other veggies you need to use up

Instructions

  1. If you need to chop and fry off any protien, do so in a pan now with any addins you like such as garlic or chilli
  2. Now, boil water for your ramen, enough to use this as our base
  3. Get your ramen simmering with any flavour sachets that come with it
  4. while this cooks mix your kewpie and yolk together with a mini whisk. slowly add water from the cooking ramen to temp our mixture until thinner and emulsified
  5. use the ramen has just about finished, add your yolky mixture, protien and any veggies
  6. give it all a minute to get to know each other on a simmer and then transfer to a serving bowl
  7. top with spring onion and crispy onions and your favourite chilli oil
  8. ENJOY
